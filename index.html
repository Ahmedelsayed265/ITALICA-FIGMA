About Us

Marble flooring and granite countertops. Quartz cladding and Onyx tables. Elegant, luxurious home living and long-lasting, sensible construction. Italica is committed to delivering beautiful houses and affordable homes. Our activities include importing some of the premier granite and marble in the world, selling to wholesale and retail clients, and taking our team of talented craftsmen to complete both large- and small-scale projects.
